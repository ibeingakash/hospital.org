About Us

MEDICOVER is a health care institution providing patient treatment with specialized medical and nursing staff and medical equipment. The best-known type of medicover is the general medicover, which typically has an emergency department to treat urgent health problems ranging from fire and accident victims to a sudden illness. A district medicover typically is the major health care facility in its region, with many beds for intensive care and additional beds for patients who need long-term care. Specialized medicover include trauma centers, rehabilitation medicover, children's medicover, seniors' (geriatric) medicover, and medicover for dealing with specific medical needs such as psychiatric treatment (see psychiatric medicover) and certain disease categories. Specialized medicover can help reduce health care costs compared to general medicover. medicover are classified as general, specialty, or government depending on the sources of income received. A teaching medicover combines assistance to people with teaching to medical students and nurses. A medical facility smaller than a medicover is generally called a clinic. medicover have a range of departments (e.g. surgery and urgent care) and specialist units such as cardiology. Some medicover have outpatient departments and some have chronic treatment units. Common support units include a pharmacy, pathology, and radiology. medicover are usually funded by public funding, health organisations (for profit or nonprofit), health insurance companies, or charities, including direct charitable donations. Historically, medicover were often founded and funded by religious orders, or by charitable individuals.
